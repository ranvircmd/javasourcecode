Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Is JavaSourceCode really free?

Yes! JavaSourceCode is 100% free for all students. We believe in providing quality education accessible to everyone. All courses, notes, and video lectures are available without any cost.

Which universities are covered?

We primarily cover AKU and BUE universities, but our content is useful for students from any university. Our notes and courses follow standard curriculum patterns used across 100+ universities in India.

How do I download notes?

Simply navigate to the Notes section, use filters to find your subject/semester, and click the download button on any note card. The PDF will start downloading immediately. No registration required!

Are video lectures available offline?

Video lectures are currently available for online streaming. We're working on adding offline download capabilities. For now, you can download PDF notes for offline study.

How often is content updated?

We update our content regularly - usually every semester. New notes, courses, and video lectures are added based on latest curriculum changes and student requests.

Can I contribute content?

Yes! We welcome contributions from students and educators. If you have quality notes or want to help, please contact us through the Contact section. We review all submissions before publishing.

How to Search

Use the search bar or filters to find specific courses and notes. You can filter by Course Type (B.Tech/BCA/BBA), Branch, and Semester for precise results.

  • Select your course type first
  • Choose your branch
  • Select semester
  • Use search for specific topics

Voice Search

Click the microphone icon in the navigation bar and speak your query. The system will automatically search for relevant content.

Tips: Speak clearly and mention the subject name for best results.

Downloading Content

Click on any course or note card to view details. Use the download button to save PDF notes or access video lectures.

  • Browse or search for content
  • Click on the card
  • Hit the download/view button
  • Enjoy learning!
